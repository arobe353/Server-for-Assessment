About Me

Hi! I'm Milly, a teenage artist from Brisbane, Australia. I love to draw, but not just that! I'm also in the process of writing multiple books, and I love graphic design - specifically web design. This website was made for my Information Technology assessment on coding in HTML and CSS.

I have 2 siblings, 2 dogs, and a cat, and as you might have seen from the website colour, my favourite colour is seafoam green! When I'm not writing, drawing, or coding, I play soccer as an attacking midfield or striker, and I also play Dungeons and Dragons.

When it comes to art, I love character design and have a semi-cartoon art style. I especially love drawing people's D&D characters, as they're usually very creative and have interesting personalities.

You might know me on other platforms as: @pixxeidust , @PixxeIDust (with a capital i instead of L), @PixxelDust , @arobe353 , or @QueenOfKittehCatz
